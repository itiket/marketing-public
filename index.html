Our Proud Features

Your complete toolkit for event success!

Custom Website Builder

Create beautifully branded event pages with customizable layouts, detailed schedules, maps, images, and registration forms — no coding required.

Advanced Event Management

Manage multiple events, categories, pricing tiers, and recurring schedules. Add detailed descriptions, dates, locations, and registration capacities.

Flexible Registration & Ticketing

Support for multiple ticket types (early bird, group, VIP), registration transfer, participant self-service portal, and QR-code check-in.

Coupons, Discounts & Promotions

Create limited-time offers, promo codes, and bundled discounts to boost registrations and reward loyal attendees.

Seamless Payment Processing

Accept payments via credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Gopay, OVO, DANA), bank transfers, and other global/local methods with secure gateways.

Built-In Analytics & Reporting

Gain insights into attendee behavior, sales trends, and marketing effectiveness with comprehensive reports and dashboards.

Participant Management Tools

Manage registrations, changes, refunds, team formations, communication (email/SMS/WhatsApp), and profile updates from one portal.

Marketing & Engagement Features

Email campaigns, social media sharing, SEO-friendly event pages, in-app messages, and abandoned cart recovery to drive engagement.

Safety & Compliance Tools

Capture waivers, emergency contact info, and manage legal agreements seamlessly during registration
